Music for you and us

There are a couple of songs in my playlist that remind me of you or us. Here is a list of some of them! Think of it as a playlist I made for you. If you click on the image, it'll take you to the song on Youtube! :)

Washing Machine Heart

by Mitski

I was never really into Mitski, I kinda hated her but I got into this song because you've played it a couple of times. And now I like listening to it because it reminds me of you.

Co2

by Prateek Kuhad

I like this song because it sounds so soft and sweet. I like the whole lyric of deserving each other and the metaphors he uses.

Terrified

by Jazmin Bean

This was the song I had on replay when I was getting to know you. I was terrified of what could come from our relationship and how I would be treated.

don't be so hard on your own beauty

by yeule

I listened to this song often a couple weeks after we were official. Because of my past experiences, I was scared to open up to you. Then I realized that I felt safe to be around you.

Would You Fall in Love with Me Again

by Jorge Rivera-Herrans, Anna Lee

This one might be a little cringe since it's from a musical I think, but it sounds so pretty and sad. Because no matter what you do or where you'll go, I'll always love you.

We'll Never Have Sex

by Leith Ross

I chose this song because of the lyrics. I think it's self explanatory.

Lo Que Siento

by Cuco

I love the mix of Spanish and English in this song. It's a nice little love song.

love like that

by Endie

A fun upbeat song about feeling a different kind of love on the first date! Kinda like us!

Nothing

by Bruno Major

This song sounds so mellow, it reminds me of how romantic you are at times.

Waltz

by lilrosebush

As the title and lyrics suggest, this song gives like princess dance kinda vibes. It sounds majestic, especially since it's a man's point of view.

Si Me Voy

by Cuco, The Marias

This song is in Spanish but it's about going wherever you're going. Because of you, I'm me, and I want to be wherever you are.

Die With A Smile

by Lady Gaga, Bruno Mars

Of course this song would remind me of us! We have played and sung along to it together.

I remember how your hands felt on mine

by Wisp

This song reminds me of you, literally in the title and the lyrics.

Apple Cider

by beabadoobee

The song and lyrics for some reason brings me back to when we went to the apple farm together. It gives me feelings of fall, orange leaves, and chilly but sunny weather.

From the Start

by Good Kid

I love that you got into Good Kid, and I think it was through this song. Nonetheless, I love singing this song with you.

Dreaming Of You

by Selena

This one is kind of old, but as the name suggests, this songs feels like when I'm dreaming of you.

Archie, Marry Me

by Alvvays

Take me to the court house and sign some silly little papers with me. How bout it?

Pintura

by Patio Solar

This song is in Spanish and I understand it as just enjoying each other's presence and trying to capture it in a painting. Makes me think of going on a picnic with you and watching the clouds go by.

Orbit

by Good Kid

A fun little Good Kid song that we've listened to before. Reminds me how I just love being around you and just being in your presence.

digital crush

by Powfu, Ouse, Ryan Librada

I think it's a silly song about playing games online which is kind of how we started. Unlike what the song says, yes I'm yours, and yes you're hot.
