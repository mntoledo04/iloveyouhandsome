Our future

The future is always uncertain, but I'd like to think that there a couple of things that we will probably do or have. No matter what the future holds for us, here's a list of things I think would happen later in our lives.

1. We'll probably have a small wedding. It'll be cute and fun!

2. I'll often have to make you foods that will sit right in your stomach when you have tummy troubles.

3. We'll adopt a cute little Cockateil and he'll somehow tame Patty.

4. I'm going to say 'No' a lot when we go grocery shopping and you want something you shouldn't have.

5. We'll sleep early on weekdays and sleep later on weekends but not too late.

6. We might wake up early on weekends, but we'll stay in bed for another hour.

7. I'm definitely going to be drooling on your chest.

8. We'll play music on our cleaning days and we'll dance together.

9. You'll be gaming while I'll be drawing in the same room.

10. Grocery shopping will be something we'll do together.

11. Our anniversaries will be a competition as to who can surprise the other one first.

12. I'll find a way to make you eat breakfasts that you can enjoy.

13. Movie nights are gonna be SUPER cozy and we will make pillow forts.

14. We'll find ways to be childish. Pillow fights, jokes, running away from each other, you name it.

15. We'll still be playing games together.
